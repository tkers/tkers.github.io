Thornfell InvisiClues

If you're stuck at some point in Thornfell Manor: 1984, find the question that most pertains to your problem. Click the masked box to reveal the first hint. If you are still stumped, go on to the next hint. The invisible hints generally progress from a gentle nudge in the right direction to a full answer. Avoid reading too far ahead, as some questions might spoil the mystery.

Good luck, detective!
